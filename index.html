The Film Fetcher

By Rasmi R. and Joseph M.

The Film Fetcher dynamically renders a list of recent movie releases on the web page. Clicking on a movie will bring you to a new page that displays information about the movie as well as critic and user reviews. A history of movies you've viewed is stored in local storage and rendered on the right-hand side of the screen on the home page. Styling was done using Bulma CDN.
